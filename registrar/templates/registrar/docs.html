How do I mark payments that have been deposited?
Head to the payments page. You will be presented with a list of deposits that have not yet been deposited. Next to each one is a checkbox ( ). Click the checkbox next to every payment that has been deposited. Then click the Deposit button.

How do I add a new camp registration or new TIFD membership?
The only way to enter a camp registration or membership renewal is to use the public facing online form.

If you have a paper form, it has to be manually entered. If certain things like E-mail or phone are missing, use either your email or registrar@tifd.org. Keep in mind the registration form will try and email them a reciept so be careful if you decide to enter a random email address.

How do I process a payment?
PayPal registrations are fully automated. The only time you should need to process a payment is with a non-PayPal payment.

The registrar's HomePage will have a list of registrations that need attention. Pick the registration that you have a payment for and hit the Add Payment button. The Add Payment button calculates the proper amount owed based on the shopping cart and makes a lot of assumptions about what is going on in order to make things easier. Simply entering the amount and check number should be sufficient for most cases.

If you need to so some custom itemizations, click the dropdown button ( ) and pick the add itemized payment button.

Once the payment has been entered, the registration will still have to be approved.
How do I approve a registration and what does that mean?
Once a registration is fully paid it will have a button called Approve Registration Click that button and you will be taken to a page that displays the registration conifirmation email. You can edit the email if you like and add a custom note. Then hit Save and the registration is complete! The member's TIFD membership dates will be displayed and their expiration date will be included in the confirmation email.
How do I edit or change a camp registration or membership renewal?
Head on over to the master list of registrations. Make sure you have the right year selected in the upper right corner.
  • Membership registrations will be prefaced with "MEMBERSHIP".
  • Camp registrations will be prefaced with "CAMP".
To the left of the registration you want to change, click the dropdown button ( ) and select Edit registration. You will see the same interface the registrant saw when they registered and you can change anything needed.
How do I look up a member or search the database?
Head on over to the list of members in good standing. By default it will show all current members. You can use the search box to search for members by first or last name.
